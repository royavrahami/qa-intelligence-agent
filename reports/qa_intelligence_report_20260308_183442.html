▸ The system offers two modes of content presentation, BALANCED and COOL, to cater to different consumer sensitivities.

Retrieval-Augmented Generation with Covariate Time Series
🤖 GenAI & LLMs
The paper discusses the extension of Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) techniques to Time-Series Foundation Models (TSFMs), specifically in the context of predictive maintenance for industrial applications like the Pressure Regulating and Shut-Off Valve (PRSOV). The authors introduce RAG4CTS, a novel framework that addresses challenges such as data scarcity and transient sequences by using a hierarchical time-series knowledge base and a bi-weighted retrieval mechanism. This approach enhances prediction accuracy and has been successfully deployed in a real-world scenario at China Southern Airlines, identifying faults without false alarms.
